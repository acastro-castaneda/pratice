 About The Pieces:

 -The Kings: can move one square any direction

 -The Queens:can move anywhere any direction but they can't jump over pieces

 -The Rooks: can move horizontally or vertically any number of squares

 -The Bishops: can move diagonally any number of squares

 -The Knights: moves in a "L" shape, its the only piece able to jump over other pieces

 -The Pawns: can move one square forward. Can move vertically to capture opponents.

 -If Pawn reaches the otherside of board it can be promoted to any piece except king.
